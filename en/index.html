The Current Problem: Democratization
The global numismatics market is experiencing remarkable growth. This expansion is explained by the increasing interest of investors in tangible assets in times of economic uncertainty. The numismatics market, although rich in history, is often perceived as opaque, illiquid, and reserved for an elite. Authentication is complex, transactions are slow, and entry barriers are high for new collectors and investors.
Our Revolutionary Solution
NumisVR uses blockchain technology to tokenize certified collectible coins. Each NFT represents a share of ownership of a rare coin, stored securely. We have a vault located in the Geneva Free Port, recognized worldwide for its inviolability. Our offices are in Switzerland, Geneva. This democratizes access, guarantees authenticity through immutable records, and brings unprecedented liquidity to the market.
Added Value
Invest from a few dollars: Fractional tokenization allows acquiring a share of a rare coin without having to buy the object in its entirety, which was previously reserved for a wealthy elite. Opening up to a global audience: Collectors and investors from around the world can participate, regardless of their place of residence or budget. Flexibility of use: tokens allow access to museum services and events 24/7 on our digital platform, making the numismatics market more liquid and accessible than the traditional market.

Security and trust

​NumisVR only accepts coins from our certified numismatic partners, as well as coins certified by PCGS and NGC, recognized worldwide by all numismatic houses.

​For security reasons and to offer the greatest transparency to its users and investors, NumisVR has chosen to work only with numismatic coins certified by the two main international companies:
PCGS (Professional Coin Grading Service) and NGC (Numismatic Guaranty Corporation).

These certifications are integrated and recognized by all major numismatic houses in the world, guaranteeing rigorous authenticity and precise evaluation of the coins. This universal recognition makes PCGS and NGC the unavoidable reference standards for numismatic certification, ensuring optimal traceability and effective protection against counterfeiting.

This requirement allows NumisVR to ensure the security of transactions, the protection of coins thanks to inviolable slabs, and to offer a high level of trust to the entire community.

Thus, each PCGS or NGC certified coin is authenticated, graded with international rigor, encapsulated in a secure case, and benefits from a unique number allowing online verification.

By working only with these two types of certified coins, NumisVR guarantees transparency, security, and optimal valorization for collectors and investors.

LEGAL STRUCTURE OF NUMISVR AFTER ICO

We will create a double structure to guarantee our clients the best services:

Layer 1: Digital

Creation of a legal entity in Estonia, whose role will be the following:

Obtaining a financial license (VASP) to ensure NFT exchanges in compliance with the laws in force throughout Europe.

Creation and verification of accounts with appropriate KYC studies.

Global management of the platform (NFT transfers, billing, user accounts).

Layer 2: Physical

Creation of a legal entity in Switzerland, whose role will be the following:

Management of a secure vault in the Free Port of Geneva.

Receipt of coins and authentication certificates.

Management of the removal of coins for holders of the complete range of NFTs.

Physical audit of the value of the coins updated.
