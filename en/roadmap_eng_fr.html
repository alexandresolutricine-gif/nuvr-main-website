NUMISVR

Numismatic Tokenization - Our vision, step by step

About the project

The NUMISVR project adopts a phased deployment strategy, aiming for security, scalability, and progressive adoption of numismatic coin tokenization, with clear steps and tangible benefits at each milestone.

Key development stages

1

Foundation (Q3 2025)

Official website launch and brand building:

  • Launch of the website presenting the team, vision, and roadmap
  • Creation of graphic identity and project branding

Community animation:

  • Opening and management of official channels on X (Twitter), Telegram, and Discord
  • Setting up initial community exchanges, FAQ, newsletters

Documentation and transparency:

  • Publication of whitepaper and detailed technical documentation

Marketing launch:

  • First promotional campaigns targeting collectors, investors, and enthusiasts
2

Presale & Early Access (Q3 - Q4 2025)

NUMISVR token (NUVR) presale:

  • Presale rollout through successive pricing tiers
  • Selection of first partners and numismatic ambassadors

Security and compliance:

  • First security audit of smart contracts and KYC/AML platform

Strategic partnerships:

  • Formation of collaborations with experts, numismatic institutions, and blockchain companies
3

ICO Launch (Q3 2025 - Q1 2026)

Public token sale:

  • Official launch of global sale with automatic distribution
  • Publication of participation statistics and transparent fund management

Feature deployment:

  • Launch of user tools: wallets, purchase/management interface
  • Integration of digital proof of ownership (certificates, HD photos, history)

Testing and feedback:

  • Collection of user feedback for rapid iterations and fixes
4

Ecosystem Expansion (Q4 2025 - Q1 2026)

Launch of centralized marketplace:

  • Dedicated platform for tokenized numismatic coins with optimized UX/UI
  • Interoperability with crypto wallets (SPL / solana) and advanced security protocols
  • KYC/AML modules for compliance, testing phase, and complete audits

Market opening:

  • Procedures for token listing on major crypto exchanges (CEX & DEX)
  • Integration solutions (APIs, SDK) for specialized partners

Partner project onboarding:

  • Relations with insurance companies, logistics, and numismatic expertise
  • Complementary services: coin insurance, secure storage, remote expertise

International campaigns:

  • Marketing amplification in other regions (US, Asia), local events
5

Consolidation & New Services (Q1 - Q3 2026)

Creation of Numismatic VR NFTs:

  • Development of an immersive virtual museum to showcase tokenized coins
  • VR navigation with thematic rooms, 3D modeling, and interactive tours
  • Blockchain certification with NFT guaranteeing authenticity and traceability
  • Private spaces for collectors and public areas for discovery

Optimized and scalable management:

  • Internal secondary market, fractional ownership, automated reporting
  • Continuous monitoring of legal compliance and regulatory adaptations

Community support and innovation:

  • Multilingual customer service, educational animations, and contests
  • Evolving roadmap based on user feedback and market analysis

Assessment and next steps:

  • Progress report and preparation of new development phases
  • Opening to other categories of collectible objects and asset classes

Update – August 2025

Imminent launch of Phase 1 with the finalization of brand identity and opening of the first community channels. Strategic partnerships are under negotiation.

Final Objective

To enable the democratization and liquidity of collectible coins through blockchain, while guaranteeing security, transparency, and the connection between physical and digital worlds.
